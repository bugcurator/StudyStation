Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Study Station ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We prioritize minimal data collection to respect your privacy. Study Station is primarily an informational site that links to external resources. We do not collect personal data, IP addresses, cookies, or tracking information directly from users.

If analytics are implemented, we may collect non-personal usage data such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring source (e.g., which site linked you to us)
  • Device type and operating system

3. Personal Data

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly via email. Any information shared through correspondence will be used only to respond to your inquiry.

4. Use of Information

Any usage data collected is used solely for:

  • Improving website performance and content
  • Monitoring traffic and user engagement
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues

We do not sell, share, or use personal data for marketing or advertising.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Study Station does not use custom tracking cookies. However, third-party sites linked from our platform may use their own cookies and trackers. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before engaging with their content.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies before interacting with them.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect any minimal data we collect. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Study Station is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Users

If you are accessing Study Station from outside Pakistan, your data may be transferred to and processed in Pakistan. By using our site, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Please check back periodically.

11. Contact Us
